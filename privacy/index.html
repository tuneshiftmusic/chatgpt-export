Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

ChatGPT Export is a Chrome extension that exports ChatGPT conversations to Markdown files saved directly on your device.

Data collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All processing happens entirely within your browser on your device.

Permissions

The extension requests access to chatgpt.com and chat.openai.com solely to read the conversation text displayed on the page and convert it into a Markdown file. That file is saved directly to your device — it is never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

Third parties

No data is shared with any third party. The extension contains no analytics, tracking, advertising, or external network requests of any kind.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Open an issue on GitHub.
